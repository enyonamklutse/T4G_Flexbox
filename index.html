About Us

Our alumni are a proud reflection of our school's enduring commitment to academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning.
As graduates, they represent a diverse community of leaders, innovators, entrepreneurs, professionals, and changemakers making meaningful contributions across various industries and communities around the world.
Our alumni recently embarked on an exciting educational and recreational trip to Shai Hills, one of Ghana's most treasured nature reserves.
The excursion provided an excellent opportunity for graduates to reconnect, strengthen friendships, and create lasting memories while exploring the rich natural beauty and cultural heritage of the area.
